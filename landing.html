Questions you might have

Does it work over USB?

Currently WiFi only. USB support is planned for the Premium tier to achieve even lower latency.

Can I use it with an iPad?

The web client works on any browser including Safari on iPad. However, iOS has some limitations with touch forwarding — Android is recommended for the best experience.

Is it as good as SuperDisplay or Spacedesk?

SuperDisplay has a kernel-level driver which gives it an edge in latency. MirrorX is open source, cheaper, and doesn't require a tablet app install. It's designed for productivity — coding, reading, writing — not gaming.

What about my privacy?

MirrorX runs entirely on your local network. Your screen content never goes to the internet. No telemetry, no analytics, no accounts. The code is open source — you can verify this yourself.
